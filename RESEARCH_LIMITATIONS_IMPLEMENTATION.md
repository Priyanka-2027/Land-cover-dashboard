# Research Limitations Implementation - Final Academic Touch

## 🎓 Overview

This implementation adds a comprehensive **Research Limitations** section to demonstrate academic integrity and methodological transparency - exactly what faculty expect in high-quality research projects.

## ✅ Implementation Details

### 1. Dashboard Integration
- **Location**: Tab 2 (Prediction & Simulation) after Model Details
- **Visual Design**: Two-column layout with warning styling
- **Content**: Technical and model constraints clearly separated

### 2. Report Generator Integration
- **New Section**: `_generate_limitations_section()`
- **Position**: After Methodology, before Conclusions
- **Length**: ~3,200 characters of detailed limitations
- **Academic Style**: Professional research paper format

### 3. Land Cover Analysis Integration
- **Additional Constraints**: Specific to classification analysis
- **Export Integration**: Limitations included in downloadable reports
- **Visual Presentation**: Organized in information boxes

## 📋 Limitations Covered

### Technical Limitations
- ✅ **Image Quality Dependency**: Analysis accuracy varies with resolution
- ✅ **NDVI Approximation**: RGB-based instead of true NIR spectral data
- ✅ **Temporal Resolution**: Limited to available timestamps

### Model Constraints  
- ✅ **Training Dataset**: Model trained on limited EuroSAT dataset
- ✅ **Linear Assumption**: Prediction assumes linear trends
- ✅ **Patch-based Analysis**: May miss fine-scale spatial patterns

### Methodological Boundaries
- ✅ **Spatial Scale**: Constrained to input image resolution
- ✅ **Validation**: Statistical validation without ground-truth
- ✅ **Uncertainty**: Limited uncertainty quantification
